What This Space Is About

✦ ◆ ✦

There's something magical about cracking open a book outside — the sun on the pages, a soft breeze, and nothing on the agenda. This page is a little corner of the internet dedicated to my two favorite things: getting lost in a great read and spending time outdoors. Whether it's a mountain trail or a blanket in the backyard, the best moments happen away from screens.
